Adding Ultimate Waterfall chart

  1. Select on 4 dots under Build Visual, then add more visuals
  2. Under search box type "Ultimate Waterfall" then add the Chart to Visual.
  3. Insert Colaberry logo and stretch it into the open space.
  4. Insert the Ultimate Waterfall chart on top of the Logo and go to Modeling and bring the waterfall forward
  5. Go to visualization general effects background and increase the transparency to 19%
  6. Build Visual:  Values  insert the followings "Total Enrollments % increase vs.Prev Classes",  "Daily Enrollments % increase vs.Prev Classes",  "FullPayPerInc", "PartialPayPerInc","PClassVsAvg","OHPreVsAvg","OHPostVsAvg","EBSPreVsAvg","EBSPostVAvg". After you inserting the above measurements change their names as following

       Prj

       Partial Bill

       OH Pre
