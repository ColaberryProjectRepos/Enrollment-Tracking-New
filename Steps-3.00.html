Executive Summary


David needed to know how many students were enrolling, which events brought in more students, and the difference in numbers between each class start date, and which areas were struggling.

Assembled Power BI Team to build a Dashboard that Display the necessary Information, Gauging enrollment data provided 

However, we soon encountered several obstacles that threatened to derail the progress.

Challenges :

  1. The first problem was grouping and reformatting the CSD. The data was disorganized and in a format that made it difficult to analyze. The team worked tirelessly to clean and arrange the data, using their expertise in data manipulation to create a format that would allow them to effectively analyze the data.                    
  2. Next, the team faced the challenge of calculating projection total enrollments using variables. After much trial and error, the team discovered a new statistical model that solved the problem and allowed them to create accurate projections.                              
  3. In the end, the team had overcome all of the obstacles in their way and successfully reformatting and analyzing the CSD. They learned the importance of teamwork and determination in the face of adversity, and their efforts helped the company make informed decisions based on accurate data analysis.
